Missing Link

Inspired by the Space.com headline 'Missing link' black hole found? this kinetic typography animation transforms a news headline into a visual exploration of cosmic mystery. The design uses dynamic text movement and layered depth to reflect the mysterious and expansive nature of a black hole.

Scope:

  • Motion Graphic

  • Kinetic Typography

  • Key-Frame Animation

  • Sound Design

Software:

  • Adobe After Effects
