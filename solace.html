Solace

What is the meaning of home? Why do people long to have one at all? Can those who leave their family homes ever truly find one of their own?

I've been asking those questions my nomad self for several years, before I made this book - an exploration of the idea of home as an inner feeling that embodies freedom, safety, and personal ground rules.

This book features my original writing, design, and photography. I utilized a minimalistic duocolor design to enhance the emotional resonance of the visuals, creating a compelling narrative that complements the theme. Each element — layout, binding, and print — was meticulously designed to evoke the intricate home-to-mind relationship, culminating in a unique, tactile experience.
