Animation Principles Series

This series of six short conceptual animations is a visual homage to key Disney animation principles: Arc, Pose-to-Pose, Secondary Action, Staging, Squash and Stretch, and Exaggeration. Each segment is unified by a consistent aesthetic, exploring its own aspect of movement.

Scope:

  • Motion Graphic

  • Key-Frame Animation

  • 3D Rendering

Software:

  • Adobe After Effects

  • Adobe Illustrator
