Urban Solitude

The project required to design and create a physical book based on the content from a Wikipedia article - The Moon.

For the book, I chose a minimalist aesthetic to underscore the complexity of the subject.

The final copy was created using the exposed binding technique, allowing for a fully opened spread design.
