Urban Solitude

In the bustling metropolis of New York City, everyone deserves a moment of respite amidst the chaos. This campaign was created to highlight the significance of private spaces and quietness, inviting New York City residents to escape the endless city hustle during a series of silent hour events in various public locations.

Scope:

  • Social Campagn

  • Photography

  • Graphic Design

  • Video Editing

  • Color Correction

Software:

  • Adobe Illustrator

  • Adobe Photoshop

  • Adobe Premiere Pro

Design concept

The project required a variety of technical aspects to bring the campaign to life. We utilized photo-based designs and motion-photography to create compelling visuals that capture the essence of tranquility amidst the city's chaos. Subway-style locations were chosen for their familiarity and accessibility, enhancing the campaign's reach.

Additionally, a promotional video was produced with a film aesthetic, adding a cinematic touch to the campaign.

The entire project, from concept to execution, was completed within a two-week timeframe.
