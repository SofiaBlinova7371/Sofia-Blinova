Glasses

Through this animation, I delve into the concept of metaphorical rose-colored glasses that hide the true nature of our world.

As a conceptual film, 'Glasses' possesses a distinctive aesthetic of imperfect dystopia, through which I hope to immerse viewers in reflections on the choice between embracing the often challenging reality or remaining in the cozy yet illusory comfort.

Scope:

  • Key-frame animation

  • Motion Graphics

  • Video Editing

  • Sound Design

Software:

  • Adobe Illustrator

  • Adobe Photoshop

  • Adobe After Effects

  • Adobe Premiere Pro

Festivals & Awards

“Glasses” successfully made it through the selection process for the 2023 Film Festival at UC Davis and the Aggiemation Film Festival 2022, recieving an award for Best Experimental.
