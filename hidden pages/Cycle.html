Everything is a Cycle

"Everything is a Cycle" is an animation that marked the beginning of my journey into the world of short films.

Inspired by the conclusion of the COVID-19 pandemic and the onset of a new era of our society, the film illustrates the interconnections of everything in this world.

The animation was hand-drawn digitally within 3 weeks and included over 2000 individual frames.

Scope:

  • Animation

  • Illustration

  • Video Editing

  • Sound Design

Software:

  • Toon Boom Harmony

  • Adobe Illustrator

  • Adobe Photoshop

  • Adobe After Effects

Festivals & Awards

“Everything was a Cycle” premiere took place at the NYU Student Film Festival in summer 2022.

It also made it through the selection for the Aggiemation Film Festival 2022, and was awarded a award for the best aesthetic.
