Flavor Atlas

Flavor Atlas is a data-driven series of collections centered around the flavor experiences found in herbs and spices from across the globe. The series highlights the importance of sensory experiences in our lives, offering comprehensive insights into the rich cultural, historical, and sensory significance of these ingredients. It combines data-driven collections with a sensory exploration, allowing users to discover the depth of flavors and their origins.

Flavor Atlas was created for those who wish to unlock the full spectrum of life through vibrant and diverse tastes and aromas.

Inside the Box

The print portion of the Flavor Atlas project includes a series of thematic card collections, each focusing on specific herbs and spices, and organized by unique subthemes. Each edition comes with a branded package, thematic brochure, index catalogue, and a set of corresponding spice cards.

This edition serves as a tactile resource for culinary enthusiasts and those seeking to expand their flavor horizons with exclusive ingredients. The print materials allow users to engage with the world of flavor in a sensory and visually appealing way, making it both educational and inspiring.

Website

The digital aspect of Flavor Atlas extends the project into an interactive platform, designed to enhance users' understanding and exploration of spices and their sensory profiles. The digital experience includes a comprehensive flavor spectrum, which is a JSON-based infographic that visually maps the flavor profile of each curated spice.

The website also includes detailed information about each spice, from its origins to its culinary uses, all presented in an engaging, easy-to-navigate format.
