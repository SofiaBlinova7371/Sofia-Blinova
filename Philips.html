Philips Retro Ad

As part of this project, I recreated a retro Philips stereo radio poster from the late 1960s and brought it to life as a motion graphic. This animation was developed as part of a contemporary advertising campaign that taps into the nostalgia micro trend.

The original poster was carefully reimagined scene by scene in Adobe Illustrator, with subtle adjustments to enhance animation flow and maintain visual harmony.

Scope:

  • Motion Graphic

  • Key-Frame Animation

  • Advertising

  • Sound Design

Software:

  • Adobe After Effects

  • Adobe Illustrator
